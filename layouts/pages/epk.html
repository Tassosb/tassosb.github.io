BIO

Tassos Bareiss writes songs where every word matters, where imagery conjures worlds rich with symbolism, where the first line might foreshadow the last. With a voice that resonates with the depth of his lyrics and self-assured musicianship featuring nimble finger-picking, Tassos delivers performances that have been convincing concert-goers he is a singer-songwriter worth paying attention to.


Originally from the Northeast, Tassos lives in Albuquerque, NM and finds joy, connection, and challenge in the local music scene there. He won the Love Song for Las Cruces songwriting contest in 2023.
