About

I am a Fulbright Scholar with a degree in Mathematics from the Claremont Colleges. I love digging into problems that don't present immediate answers, especially in the context of working on meaningful projects with other people. I hunger for that moment of epiphany, the breakthrough that often comes when it's least expected.

Whether it be a song or a program to visualize trends in pop song lyrics, I am always in the process of creating something. I find joy in putting the pieces together and iteratively refining my approach.

My journey into software development started with teaching myself Ruby. I quickly became obsessed with writing algorithms to solve logic puzzles and modeling my code off best practices I learned here and on sites like Codewars and Stack Overflow.

To accelerate my education, I attended a selective coding bootcamp called App Academy. I enjoyed pair programming and learning the intricacies of tools like React and Rails so much that I joined the instructional staff right after finishing the course.

During my time at App Academy I witnessed time and time again the synergy that results from thoughtful, deliberate collaboration. I'm looking to join a team that invests in the collaborative process and is excited about the problems they are working on.
